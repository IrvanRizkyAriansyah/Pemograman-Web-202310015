PENDENGAR BULANAN

Kunto Aji is one of Indonesia’s male singer/songwriter. He released his album called “Generation Y” in 2015. In September 2018, Kunto Aji released his sophomore album called “Mantra, Mantra”. Kunto Aji’s second album captures many people’s attention not only his fans. One of the things that may give an effect of this positive critic is because his songwriting had grown tremendously. In “Mantra, Mantra” he worked with 4 different producers; Ankadiov, Uga Swastadi, Petra Sihombing and Bam Mastro. Kunto Aji’s second album talks and spread the awareness of how important mental health is. His lead singles “Konon Katanya” reminds people to chase their own dreams despite what other people might say, “Topik Semalam” is a message about a guy who hasn’t proposed to his longtime girlfriend but asking her to be patient because he has his plan to do so, and also “Rehat” a self-reminder to people that it’s completely okay to take a breather for a while. The album went on to receive 5 nominations Anugerah Musik Indonesia (AMI) Awards in 2019 including Best Pop Male Solo Act and Best Album.

HINDIA

1,530,828

PENDENGAR BULANAN

Hindia is the moniker of Baskara Putra, also known as the vocalist and songwriter/producer of Indonesian music group .Feast. After releasing both of the band’s heavily polarized MULTIVERSES (2017) and Beberapa Orang Memaafkan (2018), Hindia went on his own venture starting with the release of the collaboration-heavy No One Will Find Me (2018) and Tidak Ada Salju Di Sini (2018). Some might say the ‘true’ start of his own solo career is through the release of Evaluasi in March 2019, which was then suceeded by a constant stream of singles such as Secukupnya, Jam Makan Siang (ft. Matter Mos ), Membasuh (ft. Rara Sekar ), Dehidrasi and Apapun Yang Terjadi , which are all part of Hindia’s debut album titled Menari Dengan Bayangan (November 2019), then included in 2019 album of the year lists by many Indonesian media and publications such as Billboard Indonesia, Kompas, Tempo and Pop Hari Ini. Amassing more than 1 million monthly listeners on Spotify alone in less than nine months, Hindia was nominated as one of the nation’s best newcomer (AMI Awards 2019), album of the year for Menari Dengan Bayangan (AMI Awards 2020), alternative solo artist of the year (AMI Awards 2020), and song of the year for Secukupnya (ABBI 2020).

NADIN AMIZAH

PENDENGAR BULANAN

Spending her teenage years feeling isolated from the outside world, Nadin Amizah began her song-writing journey before her 17th birthday. What started as her way of putting whatever her feelings are into good use, Nadin continues singing and taking little but sure steps into the magical musical world, making friends and new stories to unfold. Nadin writes only what she feels. She will lay whatever feels like a knot on her chest or throat down on a paper or notes, carefully choosing words to describe the unsaid.

She often writes about her rampant journey on finding the true meaning of the word love, divorces she had witnessed throughout her entire short-lived life, her dreams, her imaginations, her mother, her family, and all the people who has touched souls with her along her way. Nadin gifts her surroundings with raw and unfiltered emotions. Making them who crave for their voices to be sung feel attracted to her, as they feel represented.

DANILLA

401,478

PENDENGAR BULANAN

With a voice like velvet, a back catalogue of instant classics and a penchant for unpredictability, Danilla Riyadi is one of the most compelling Indonesian music artists in recent years. Born and raised in Jakarta, Danilla 's first emerged as a demure chanteuse with Telisik , her critically-acclaimed, bossa nova-inflected 2014 debut album. Thanks to songs such as Ada Disana and Senja Diambang Pilu , Danilla would soon become an in-demand live act. For the 2017 sophomore album Lintasan Waktu, Danilla confounded expectations by going into darker territory on a collection of songs – including Aaa and Kalapuna – that saw her come into her own as a writer. The album coincided with Danilla ’s increased popularity on social media thanks to her warts-and-all online persona. She would also branch out into collaborations and acting roles, while continuing to develop musically on Fingers, the 2019 EP that she wrote, performed and produced on her own. In 2022, Danilla released POP SEBLAY, her long-awaited third album that once again saw her embrace new musical directions as heard on tracks like MPV and KIW". Inspired by and recorded with her backing band and close associates, POP SEBLAY is chock-full of musical and lyrical in-jokes while also comprising Danilla ’s strongest collection of songs to date.
